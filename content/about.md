#### Here's a meet the maker

I'm Dakota - artisan, founder and the hands behind every stitch. I work with vegetable-tanned hides and desert-tone dyes. I believe leather should be marked by time, not erased by polish. Every piece I make is meant to be carried, used and remembered.
